Description:
A block puzzle game with a twist — play on a hexagonal grid!

Drag and drop pieces from the tray onto the board to fill and clear lines. Strategize your placements to clear multiple lines at once for bonus points.

FEATURES
• Two game modes: classic 10x10 square grid and hexagonal grid
• Drag-and-drop controls
• Line clearing across rows, columns, and hex axes
• Undo your last move
• Scoring with multi-line clear bonuses
• High score tracking for each mode
• Sound effects and haptic feedback
